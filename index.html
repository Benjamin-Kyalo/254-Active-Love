Introducing "254 Active Love": Where Compassion Fuels Change! Founded in 2021, this community-based organization (CBO) is the collaborative effort of Jack, Ricky, Stephanie, and Benjamin and others. They came together with a shared vision: to create a ripple effect of positivity that starts with the youngest and most vulnerable among us.

Since its inception, 254 Active Love has been a force for good, channeling the generosity of the community...

Mission

"At 254 Active Love, we are dedicated to uplifting children's homes by providing essential items and unwavering compassion. Through our tireless efforts, we aim to create a community where no child feels forgotten or neglected, but instead feels valued and supported."

Vision

"We envision a world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, experiences love, kindness, and support. By spreading compassion and generosity, we strive to create a future where every child has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential."
