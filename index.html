ABOUT 254 ACTIVE LOVE


Introducing "254 Active Love": Where Compassion Fuels Change! Founded in 2021, this community-based organization (CBO) is the collaborative effort of Jack, Ricky, Stephanie, Benjamin, Steve and many others who came together with a shared vision: to create a ripple effect of positivity that starts with the youngest and most vulnerable among us.

Since its inception, 254 Active Love has been a force for good, channeling the generosity of the community to uplift children's homes in need. Through tireless efforts and unwavering dedication, the CBO organizes regular donation drives, collecting essential items such as clothing, school supplies, hygiene products, and toys, ensuring that every child feels cared for and supported.

254 Active Love isn't just a charitable organization; it's a family—a tight-knit community of volunteers, donors, and advocates united by a common purpose: to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others. Together, they stand as living proof that when people come together with love in their hearts, there's no limit to what they can achieve.

Join 254 Active Love as they continue to spread love, hope, and positive change throughout the community. Together, they're building a brighter future, one act of kindness at a time.
